About Me

Hi, I'm Benjamin Egonu, a dedicated UX designer transitioning into the world of software development. With a Google Certificate in UX Design and 9 years of experience as a Customer Service Executive, I bring a unique blend of user-centered design and customer service expertise to my work. Based in London, NW5, I am passionate about creating exceptional digital experiences that prioritize user needs and preferences.

My Journey

I started my career with a strong foundation in business and finance, earning a BTEC national Diploma from Kingsway College and a City and Guilds NVQ in Business Administration. My journey in UX design began with a Google Professional Certificate, where I honed my skills in user research, information architecture, and interaction design. I've completed several projects, including mobile app and website designs for Loreta's Diner & Bar and a website for Solar Farm & Panels.

Currently, I am expanding my skill set with am IBM Applied AI Professional Certificate, delving into AI and its applications. My goal is to merge my UX design expertise with software development, creating innovative and user-friendly solutions.
